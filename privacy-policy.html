Luxe-ify Privacy Policy

Last updated: January 15, 2025

Introduction

Luxe-ify ("we," "our," or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how our Chrome extension collects, uses, and protects your information when you use Luxe-ify.

Information We Collect

Local Storage Data

Luxe-ify stores the following data locally on your device using Chrome's storage API:

  • Color highlighting rules: Custom rules you create for text highlighting
  • Extension settings: Your preferences for sidebar visibility, activities collapse state, and task highlighting
  • User interface preferences: Settings for default states of UI elements

Page Content Access

Luxe-ify accesses and reads content on Current-RMS pages (*.current-rms.com) to:

  • Apply color highlighting to task-related text
  • Detect user assignments and project information
  • Enhance the user interface with collapsible sections
  • Automatically detect the current logged-in user for task assignment highlighting

How We Use Your Information

The information collected is used exclusively to:

  • Provide the core functionality of text highlighting and UI enhancements
  • Remember your preferences and settings
  • Improve your experience with Current-RMS by customizing the interface

Data Storage and Security

  • Local Storage: All your settings and preferences are stored locally on your device using Chrome's secure storage API
  • No External Servers: Luxe-ify does not send any of your data to external servers or third parties
  • No Analytics: We do not collect usage analytics, tracking data, or personal information
  • No Account Required: The extension works without requiring you to create an account or provide personal information

Data Sharing

We do not share, sell, rent, or trade your information with any third parties. All data processing occurs locally within your browser.

Your Rights and Choices

You have complete control over your data:

  • Modify Settings: You can change or delete your highlighting rules and preferences at any time through the extension popup
  • Clear Data: You can clear all extension data by removing and reinstalling the extension
  • Disable Features: You can turn off task highlighting or other features through the extension settings
  • Uninstall: Uninstalling the extension will remove all stored data from your device

Permissions Explained

Luxe-ify requests the following Chrome permissions:

  • storage: To save your preferences and highlighting rules locally on your device
  • activeTab: To interact with the current Current-RMS tab you're viewing
  • Host permission (*.current-rms.com): To run only on Current-RMS websites and provide functionality

Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page with an updated "Last updated" date. Continued use of the extension after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.

Children's Privacy

Luxe-ify is not intended for use by children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13.
